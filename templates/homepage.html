A Resource for Princeton Students Seeking Mental Healthcare

ProviderFinder is an educational platform for Princeton students designed to make the process of seeking mental healthcare easier. It aims to help students navigate the Princeton University Student Health Insurance Plan, find off-campus mental healthcare providers (if desired), and make informed choices related to seeking mental healthcare (e.g. types of therapy, what associated costs would look like, etc)

Mental Healthcare Resource Guide

A resource for those new to therapy or for those looking to explore and learn more about the US mental healthcare system

Off-Campus Provider Search

Search for off-campus providers by insurance accepted, types of therapies offered, titles/degrees, walkable from campus, specialty areas, and telehealth options
