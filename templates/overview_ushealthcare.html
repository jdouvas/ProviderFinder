Basics of US Healthcare Insurance System

Finding a mental healthcare provider often involves navigating the US healthcare insurance system. The US healthcare system, particularly US healthcare insurance, can be complicated and overwhelming to navigate. Below is a brief introduction to the US healthcare insurance structure and some important terms to know as you begin the process of finding a provider. This resource is designed to equip you with basic knowledge to aid you in finding a mental healthcare professional in an informed, cost-efficient way. However, this is by no means a comprehensive resource.
